Welcome to Inclusivity - Pride! We provide information about each sexuality under the LGBTQIA+ spectrum. Scroll through this website and visit each link to understand the respective sexuality and meaning associated with the pride flags. Enjoy the information that this website has to offer.

The terms and definitions mentioned in the website are always evolving and changing often mean different things to different people. They are provided here as a starting point for understanding.

One aspect that makes all the letters in LGBTQIA+ difficult for some people to understand is the difference between sex and gender. While sex and gender are terms that are often used interchangeably by people, they really do mean different things. Sex is all about the chromosomes, organs, and external genitalia, in simple words, its all about person's anatomy. Gender is a totally separate spectrum from sex; its more about how a person feels they best align with sociobehavioral norms or rules for each gender type.

  • Ally : A term for individuals that support and rally the cause even though they dont indentify within the community.
  • Pansexual : Someone who is attracted to people of all gender identities or someone who is attracted to a person's qualities regardless of their gender identity. The prefix "pan" itself stands for "all".
  • Cisgender : Someone whose gender identity matches the sex they were assigned at birth.
  • Questioning : This term describes someone who is not sure how they identify. Someone can be questioning their sexual orientation or gender identity.
